What is the problem we are solving (1/2)

There are lot of free CRMs in market however they don’t address Education Industry specific problems listed below. Problems for:

Educational Institutes:

  • Apart from big players, small players work on very thin margins to operate their institutes
  • Hence when it comes to technology, even after knowing its benefits, they lack investment capability and technology know-how to implement the same and reap benefits
  • Multiple tools for managing business. Increases cost of managing different tools and reduces efficiencies. Integration between different tools are also not easy.
  • Not able to analyse student’s performance and thereby a teacher’s efficiency
  • Increase reach out to new students

    What is the problem we are solving (2/2)

    Teachers of these Institutes:

    1. Most of the teachers using blackboard to explain concepts. In such cases sharing, storing and managing their classroom notes becomes tedious
    2. They spend lot of time evaluating physical answer sheet of students instead of focusing on student learning
    3. During a class session, they are unable to quickly identify if students have understood the concept or not
    4. Not able to track student performance with detailed insights

    Students of these Institutes:

    1. They spend maximum time of classroom in copying what teacher has written on the board rather than spending time in understanding the concept.
    2. In case they miss a class or 2, have to depend on other students for notes and concept clearance
